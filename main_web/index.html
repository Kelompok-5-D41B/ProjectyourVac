Kawah Putih

Ciwidey, Bandung

Kawah Putih is a tourist spot in West Java located in the village of Alam Endah, Rancabali District, Bandung Regency, West Java, located at the foot of Mount Patuha. White crater is a lake formed by the eruption of Mount Patuha. Soil mixed with sulfur around the crater is white, then the color of the water in the crater is greenish white, unique from this crater is the water sometimes changes color. If you go there, the temperature is quite cold, so many visitors who use thick jackets and scarves to get warmth.

Asia Afrika

Jl. Asia Afrika, Bandung

Jalan Asia Afrika in Bandung has a very close connection with the establishment of this Flower City. Because at that time, Governor General Herman Willem Daendels of the Netherlands stuck his stick when he ordered the founding of this city, which was enshrined as a Bandung Zero Zero monument. Evidence of the development of the city of Bandung, please take a walk to the Asia-Africa road in front of the Office of Highways and Spatial Planning of West Java Province, a small concrete peg will be found, which marks the 0 km point of the city of Bandung. This historic road is also called Bandung 0 kilometer point. The zero point is often associated with the beginning of city development. At that time the removal of the Bandung city government in the Krapyak or Dayeuh Kolot area was moved to this road in 1810. The oldest street in the city of Bandung is also close to the Cikapundung River as a source of water for city development.

Dago

Jl. Ir. H. Juanda, Bandung

Dago bandung located along Jalan Ir. H. Juanda, the road is phenomenal because there are so many snacks and at the end of the road there are also many tourist attractions. In addition, on Sundays, CFDs - Car Free Day - are routinely held along the road. of course people crowded visiting on that day. The attractions in the area include; Dutch and Japanese caves, Taman Hutan Raya, Dago Dream Park, and many cafes that are crowded for almost 24 hours. Juanda Street is in the middle of Bandung City, so the place is easily accessible by anyone. Dago is also a very 'Family Friendly' place, which means it is perfect for you to visit with your family or good relatives

Sunrise Point Cukul

Pangalengan, Bandung

Sunrise Point Cukul is one of the tourist sites in Bandung, located in the south of Bandung. Sunrise Point Cukul is claimed to be one of the best sunrise spots in Greater Bandung. When heading there, you will be served by Situ Cileunca and also a beautiful winding road.

Taman Lembah Dewata

Lembang, Bandung Barat

Taman Lembah Dewata is one of the attractions that are suitable for taking pictures and playing with family. Located in the Lembang region, Kab. West Bandung. Here you can do many activities such as cycling, horse riding, playing the maze and playing boat.

The Parlor

Cigadung, Bandung

The Parlor is a cafe located in the Cigadung area, Bandung. Located close to Dago. Here you can enjoy the many dishes that are provided, ranging from heavy food, such as pizza and burgers, to just a glass of coffee. This place is a suitable place for those of you who like to take pictures. Here there are also many local aparel who sell clothes. Often also held art exhibitions here.

The Lodge Maribaya

Lembang, Bandung Barat

The Maribaya Lodge was previously better known as The Lodge Earthbound & Adventure Park. Located in the village of Cibodas, Maribaya, not far from Lembang, Kab. West Bandung. Founded in 2005, this location initially only accommodated outbound and camping activities. It was only in 2016, The Lodge Maribaya increased creative tourism through various interesting facilities that can be enjoyed by lovers of activities outdoor. The Lodge is also an icon of Lembang, and is perfect for family purposes when the holidays arrive. So it is not strange, if during holidays this place is always crowded and sometimes creates traffic jams. But all that did not dampen The Maribaya Lodge to attract visitors. In addition to taking pictures, those of you who like camping can also spend the night there by renting a tent that is provided or is also better if you bring it yourself, of course no need to add more costs.

Museum Geologi

Jl. Diponegoro, Bandung

Museum Geologi Bandung founded on May 16, 1928. The museum has been renovated with funding from JICA (Japan International Cooperation Agency). After undergoing renovation, the Geology Museum was reopened and inaugurated by the Indonesian Vice President, Megawati Soekarnoputri on August 23, 2000. As one of the historical monuments, the museum is under the protection of the government and is a national heritage. In this museum, stored and managed abundant geological materials, such as fossils, rocks, and minerals. All of them were collected during fieldwork in Indonesia since 1850. The museum is also often a subscription of school children in educational visits. Its location in the middle of the city of Bandung facilitates access to achieve it.

Kebun Binatang

Taman Sari, Bandung

With a collection of animals more than 800 individuals from the class of mammals, aves, reptiles, and fish. Bandung Zoo has long been a favorite tourist destination for residents of Bandung & West Java. Besides functioning as a recreation area, the complete collection of animals at the Bandung Zoo, also serves as a location for education and research. Visitors who want to interact with animal collections at the Bandung Zoo are given the opportunity to take pictures with animals in certain areas. In addition, visitors can also feel the sensation of riding animals at the Bandung Zoo, Camel Camel Riding, Elephant Riding, and Horse Riding ready to serve visitors. The interaction of animals with zookeeper at the Bandung Zoo is not limited to feeding and health care, but also on animal shows. Chef parrots, blue-yellow macaws, rhinoceros hornbills, binturongs, poodles, and guinea pigs are the stars in animal shows.
